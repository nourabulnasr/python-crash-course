🌟 Introduction to Programming & Python

💻 What is Coding?

Coding is like giving instructions to a computer in a language it understands. Think of it as writing a recipe that tells your computer exactly what to do, step by step.

Real-world analogy: Just like you follow a recipe to cook a meal, computers follow code to perform tasks.

🐍 What is Python?

Python is one of the most beginner-friendly programming languages. It's named after Monty Python (the comedy group) and is designed to be readable and simple to learn.

Why Python? Simple syntax, powerful capabilities, and used by companies like Google, Netflix, and NASA.
